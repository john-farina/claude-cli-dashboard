Blackjack Rules

Basics

  • Double deck (104 cards)
  • Dealer stands on soft 17
  • Blackjack pays 3:2

Player Options

  • Hit - Draw another card
  • Stand - Keep your hand
  • Double - Double bet, receive one card
  • Split - Split matching cards (up to 4 hands)
  • Insurance - When dealer shows Ace (pays 2:1)

Hand Values

  • Number cards: face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10
  • Ace: 1 or 11
