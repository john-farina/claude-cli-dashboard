Punto Banco Rules

Betting

  • Bet on Player, Banker, Tie, or any combination
  • Player win pays 1:1
  • Banker win pays 1:1 minus 5% commission (0.95:1)
  • Tie pays 8:1
  • On a tie, Player and Banker bets push (returned)

Card Values

  • 2-9: face value
  • 10, J, Q, K: 0 points
  • Ace: 1 point
  • Hand value = sum mod 10 (only last digit)

Natural

  • If either hand totals 8 or 9 on first 2 cards, no more cards are dealt

Third Card Rules

  • Player: draws on 0-5, stands on 6-7
  • Banker: follows a fixed tableau based on their total and the Player's third card

Shoe

  • 8 decks (416 cards), reshuffled at ~50% penetration
